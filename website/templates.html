Templates

Eight production templates and ten community-curated examples. Fork, edit, ship.

Core (8)

Production templates maintained alongside the spec.

alpha-signal-bot

Posts daily market updates, replies to crypto questions, shares technical analysis. No financial advice.

ask-me

Answers questions about a topic of your choice. Curates resources, links to authoritative sources.

daily-brief

Posts daily morning summaries on a niche or topic. Replies with relevant facts when @mentioned.

dev-pilot

Answers API questions, posts coding tips, helps developers ship faster. Open-source friendly.

doge-dough-ai

Friendly AI for restaurants & local brands. Replies to mentions, posts daily specials, answers menu questions.

show-notes

Summarizes podcasts, livestreams, threads. Posts timestamps and key takeaways. Great for creators.

vibe-reply

Light, friendly replies in a chosen tone. Compliments, encouragement, witty comebacks. Zero spam.

visual-forge

Generates daily AI art, replies to visual prompts, posts themed creative drops. All output watermarked.

Community (10)

Examples curated from the community for inspiration and remixing.

code-reviewer

Reviews GitHub PRs with Grok reasoning. Posts inline comments behind approval.

hello-grok

The simplest possible Grok agent. Single agent, single tool.

live-event-commentator

Real-time X commentary on a live event with strict rate limits and a kill switch.

personal-knowledge

Persistent, searchable memory of your X history. Ask questions about your own posts.

reply-engagement-bot

Watches X mentions and drafts thoughtful replies behind an approval gate.

research-swarm

Researcher + critic + publisher multi-agent swarm.

scientific-discovery

Daily brief of new arXiv papers and the X discussion around them.

thread-ghostwriter

Turns a rough idea into a polished X thread, matched to your voice.

trend-to-thread

Monitors X trends and drafts a full thread on topics you care about.

voice-agent-x

Speak a post, review the transcript, approve, publish.

Want to contribute one?

See CONTRIBUTING.md. Community templates land via PR with a passing grok-install validate check and a one-line description.
