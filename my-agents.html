How to Use My Agents Dashboard

1. Access the dashboard
Type: @grok my agents
Or say: “Hey Grok, show me my agents”
2. Voice commands (recommended)
“Hey Grok, update all my agents”
“Hey Grok, orchestrate my agents”
“Hey Grok, what is the status of my agents?”
“Hey Grok, share my latest install”
3. See your installed agents + analytics
All agents you have installed via grok-install will appear here with live stats.
4. Orchestrate agents
Click “Link My Agents (Orchestrate)” or say “Hey Grok, orchestrate my agents”.
5. Share install cards
Use the Share button or say “Hey Grok, share my latest install”.

Tip: Voice commands work best on mobile with X voice mode. Text commands work everywhere.
